3. The Warrior's Self-Reflection: Identifying a Potential Problem

The path of a responsible warrior requires self-awareness. If you are concerned about your gambling habits or the habits of someone you know, please consider the following questions with honesty:

  • Do you find yourself spending more money or time gambling than you originally intended?
  • Have you ever hidden the extent of your gambling from people you care about?
  • Are you trying to win back money you have lost by gambling more?
  • Has gambling caused problems in your personal relationships, work, or studies?
  • Do you gamble as a way to escape from stress, boredom, or other problems in your life?
  • Have you ever sold possessions or borrowed money to fund your gambling?
  • Do you feel a sense of guilt, anxiety, or regret after gambling?
  • Do you find it difficult to stop or control your gambling, even when you want to?

If you answered "yes" to one or more of these questions, it may be a sign that you could benefit from seeking support. We urge you to speak to someone and to make immediate use of our self-exclusion tool.
